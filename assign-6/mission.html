VISSION

DAV Public School, Chandrasekharpur, Bhubaneswar envisions to create a citadel of endless learning for inquisitive minds to shoulder the responsibilities of global community and it aims to produce a Centre of Excellence by providing an environment of joyful learning. nurturing the innate potential of the learners by empowering them to overcome the upcoming challenges.

MISSION

1. To foster an atmosphere of creativity. innovation and inquisitiveness towards joyful Learning for each learner and the facilitator.
2. To awaken self-esteem through social emotional and moral obligations in order to
produce worthy citizens for our motherland 3. To set a benchmark of exemplar harmony among all the stakeholders towards the realm of perfection. 4. To practise the essence of caring and sharing by honouring the philosophy of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakkam
